Machine Learning My Way (MLMW)

Machine Learning My Way (MLMW) and Models, Data, Tools and Productisation (MDTP)

MLMW and MDTP are the two collection of notes that describe the world of machine learning from the following angles:

  • modelling concepts and techniques (the “formulas”),
  • data pipelines and workflows (the “pipelines”)
  • data acquisition, storage and operations (the “data”),
  • computer science and software tools (the “code”),
  • model and data productisation (the “money”),
  • bigger than company issues - investor sentiment, society and regulation (the “markets”)

Both collections are still incomplete, yet equipped with links to textbooks, reports, summary articles, industry cases and excercises that enable you to actually learn. Only limited number of passive learning resouces like videos or tutorials made it to the lists.
